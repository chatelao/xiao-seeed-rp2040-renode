## **RP2040-E9**

|**Reference**|RP2040-E9|
|---|---|
|**Summary**|ROM bootloader cannot boot directly into XIP cache-as-SRAM|
|**Description**|The XIP cache can be used as an additional 16kB SRAM bank when XIP caching is disabled (Section<br>2.6.3.1).
